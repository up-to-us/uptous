<strong>MYTH</strong>: Unhoused people want to live that way, that’s why they don’t better themselves.

<strong>RESPONSE</strong>: Experiencing houselessness is one of the most difficult and degrading things a person can live through. Cleaning your clothes, getting new shoes, brushing your teeth, getting reliable access to meals or taking a shower all become huge obstacles when you are on the street. What’s more, without access to these things it becomes nearly impossible to find or [maintain employment](https://nationalhomeless.org/issues/economic-justice/).
